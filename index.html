PROJECT
Built a Python full-stack project to help mute and deaf individuals practice sign language with ease. Using a CNN model, the site recognizes 31 hand signs (A-Z and 5 words). Flask powers the backend, while HTML, CSS, and JavaScript make the frontend interactive. Plus, the live camera feed lets users practice in real time, so they can nail their hand signs right on the spot. This project combines tech with accessibility, making communication more inclusive and fun!
We visited Govt. Seth Anandi Lal Poddar Badhir Sen. Sec. School in Jaipur, where we got a chance to interact with deaf and mute students through their teacher. When we showed them our hand sign recognition model, their reactions were priceless! They were super excited about the technology and loved how it could help them practice and improve their skills. It was amazing to see their enthusiasm and happiness, knowing our project could really make a difference in their learning journey.
